Safety Check

Are you working safely? Is your work environment safe?

Upstream

Production and Exploration

This sector of the oil and gas industry pertains to the identification, extraction, and production of crude oil and natural gas.

Hazards in this field include
• Asphyxiation in confined spaces
• Exposure to hazardous chemicals
• Getting struck by or caught between equipment

Midstream

Transportation

Midstream operations involve the transportation and processing of oil and natural gas.

Hazards include:
• Failure to contain flammable materials
• Explosions from excessive pressure
• Corrosion along pipeline wields

Downstream

Refining and Marketing

This sector of oil and gas production is the final production process for energy, this process is the most interactive with customer.

Hazards include:
• Failure to contain flammable materials
• Vehicle collisions in transportation
• Electrical and other hazardous tools
